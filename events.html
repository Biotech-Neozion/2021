F.A.Q

  • Who can Participate?

    Every student of life science at any stage of degree starting from bachelors to research are eligible to participate in any event. No age restriction. For any further information please contact the people below or mail us.

  • What are various events ?

    Our events range from Paper presentations, Poster/Idea presentations, Bio Debate, Bio-quiz, Bio-Hackathon, Live projects & models, and non technical events. You may visit downloads section for abstract format for paper and poster presentations. You may register using the link above or by visiting the events page. Make sure your abstracts reach us by 26th February. Event Dates are 3-4 March,2021.

  • What are the timelines ?

    Deadline for submission of abstract is 26th February, 2021. Event dates are 3-4 March 2012. Event is conducted in online mode.

    Every winner in any technical event will be presented with 1st and 2nd cash prize. In addition to that an exciting offer awaits.
